The Redbud at Heritage Inns

We built this website to offer travelers an independent view on the service and accommodation offered by the team at the Heritage Inns. We believe distinguished and high-level accommodation deserves plaudits and appreciation, and we're delighted that our resource can help guests make an informed choice of hotel.

Visitors will also have the opportunity to indulge in the friendly neighboring eaterie Giuseppe's Italian restaurant. Delight your taste buds at the Heritage Inns without even travelling beyond your front door.

Enter The Gates

Whether you're travelling for business or leisure, there's a good chance you'll find everything you need at the Heritage Inns. Perhaps you're a festival goer, or maybe flying into town for a meeting. Either way, you'll find a quiet and relaxing haven with wonderful rooms and delicious food.

The inn is situated perfectly, in close vicinity of Denton's Historic Square. Here you'll find pleasant shops and restaurants as well as landmarks including Quakertown Park and Texas Woman's University. Festivals such as Denton's very own Jazzfest or Denton 35 are also staged nearby.

You can also enjoy a delightful spa package, or if its your special day, why not consider holding a beautiful and traditional wedding at the inn?
